Visa Information

Entering Qatar

Qatar offers a streamlined visa process designed to welcome visitors from around the world. Citizens of over 100 countries can obtain a free visa on arrival or an e-visa, making Qatar one of the most accessible destinations in the Middle East.

The Hayya card, introduced during the 2022 FIFA World Cup, demonstrated Qatar's commitment to simplifying international travel. Today, Qatar continues to refine its entry processes to make your journey as smooth as possible.

Visa Types

Entry Options for Visitors

Visa on Arrival

Citizens of over 100 countries, including all GCC nationals, receive a free visa on arrival valid for up to 30 days, extendable once.

E-Visa

Apply for your Qatari e-visa online through the Ministry of Interior portal before travel. Processing typically takes 3 business days.

Transit Visa

Passengers transiting through Hamad International Airport for under 24 hours may be eligible for a complimentary transit visa.

Multiple Entry Visa

For frequent visitors or business travellers, multiple-entry visas are available for longer stays and repeated visits throughout the year.

Family Visit Visa

Residents of Qatar can sponsor family members for extended family visit visas, allowing stays of up to 6 months.

Visa Assistance

Qatar Tourism's concierge service can provide personalised assistance with visa applications and entry documentation.

Common Questions

Visa FAQs

Which nationalities receive a free visa on arrival?

Citizens of over 100 countries including all European Union member states, the United States, Canada, Australia, United Kingdom, and many Asian nations receive a complimentary visa on arrival valid for 30 days.

How do I apply for a Qatar e-visa?

Visit the Qatar Ministry of Interior's online portal at www.moi.gov.qa and complete the e-visa application form. You will need a valid passport, return flight details, and accommodation confirmation.

Can I extend my visa while in Qatar?

Yes, visas on arrival can be extended once for an additional 30 days through the Ministry of Interior's online portal or at authorised typing centres across Doha.

What documents do I need to enter Qatar?

You will need a valid passport with at least 6 months validity, a confirmed return ticket, proof of accommodation, and sufficient funds for your stay.
