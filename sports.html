Sports Nation

Sports in Qatar

A nation that has transformed itself into one of the world's premier destinations for sport.

2022 FIFAWorld Cup Host
Sports Capital

Qatar's Sporting Vision

Qatar has positioned itself at the forefront of global sport, hosting a remarkable array of world-class events. The pinnacle of this achievement was the hosting of the 2022 FIFA World Cup, the first in the Arab world, which was widely praised for its organisation and hospitality.

Beyond football, Qatar hosts the prestigious MotoGP Grand Prix at Losail Circuit, ATP and WTA tennis tournaments, world championship athletics, cycling races, and more. The Aspire Zone development has created world-class infrastructure for dozens of sports.

Major Sports & Events

Sport in Qatar

Football

Football is Qatar's most popular sport. The Qatar Stars League features numerous international stars, and Qatar's national team is rapidly developing on the world stage.

MotoGP Grand Prix

The Losail International Circuit hosts an annual MotoGP Grand Prix, with night racing under floodlights creating a unique and spectacular atmosphere.

Athletics

The Doha Diamond League is one of the most prestigious athletics meetings in the world, attracting the sport's greatest names to the Khalifa Stadium.

Golf

The Qatar Masters attracts top European Tour professionals to the challenging Doha Golf Club, which hosted the event for many years.

Equestrian Sports

Horse racing and equestrian events are deeply embedded in Qatari culture. The Qatar Racing and Equestrian Club hosts regular race meetings.

Water Sports

Qatar's warm Arabian Gulf waters provide ideal conditions for sailing, kayaking, paddleboarding, diving, and kite surfing throughout the year.
