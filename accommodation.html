Accommodation in Qatar

From ultra-luxury hotels to boutique properties, Qatar offers world-class accommodation for every style and budget.

Accommodation Types

Where to Stay in Qatar

Choose from a wide range of accommodation options that suit your travel style and budget.

Luxury

Luxury Hotels

Qatar is home to some of the world's finest luxury hotel brands including Four Seasons, Raffles, Mondrian, and The Ned, offering impeccable service and extraordinary amenities.

Business

Business Hotels

West Bay's gleaming towers house excellent business hotels with extensive conference facilities, executive services, and superb restaurants for corporate travellers.

Boutique

Boutique Properties

Intimate boutique hotels and serviced apartments throughout Doha offer a more personalised experience with local character and charm.

Resort

Beach Resorts

Qatar's coastal resorts combine pristine private beaches, sparkling pools, watersports facilities, and multiple dining options for the ultimate beach holiday.

Award WinningLuxury Destination
Accommodation Highlight

Staying at The Pearl-Qatar

The Pearl-Qatar, an artificial island off the coast of Doha, represents the pinnacle of luxury living and hospitality. Its waterfront promenades, luxury apartments, and world-class restaurants make it one of Qatar's most sought-after addresses.

Visitors staying in The Pearl enjoy easy access to hundreds of dining options, boutique and luxury shopping, and a vibrant marina atmosphere that is unique in the region.
