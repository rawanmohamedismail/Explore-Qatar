Thrill Seekers

Adventure & Sports in Qatar

Qatar's diverse landscapes offer thrilling outdoor adventures from the desert dunes to the deep blue Gulf.

Adventure Activities

Thrill Experiences in Qatar

Discover the many ways to experience adventure in Qatar's extraordinary landscapes.

Desert

Dune Bashing

Experience the adrenaline rush of off-road driving over Qatar's dramatic sand dunes with experienced desert guides in powerful 4WD vehicles.

Water

Watersports

From kayaking through mangroves to jet skiing on the Gulf, Qatar's warm coastal waters offer an extensive range of exhilarating watersports.

Active

Cycling & Running

Qatar's growing cycling infrastructure, including the Corniche path and The Pearl circuit, offers excellent routes in the cooler months.

Underwater

Scuba Diving

Qatar's clear warm waters, coral reefs, and historic pearl diving sites offer wonderful underwater adventures for divers of all experience levels.

Sport

Golf

Qatar boasts world-class golf courses including the renowned Doha Golf Club, offering challenging rounds in beautifully maintained settings.

Heritage

Desert Horse Riding

Explore Qatar's vast desert landscape on horseback with guided equestrian adventures that connect you with the land in a truly authentic way.
