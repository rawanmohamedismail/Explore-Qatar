Towns & Cities in Qatar

Qatar is more than Doha. Discover futuristic smart cities, ancient pearl-diving towns, UNESCO heritage sites, and peaceful coastal villages across this extraordinary peninsula.

Explore Beyond the Capital

Every Corner of Qatar Has a Story

Qatar's compact size — roughly the size of Connecticut — means you can explore the entire country in a single road trip. From the glittering skyline of Lusail to the ancient watchtowers of Al Khor and the UNESCO ruins of Al Zubarah, the whole peninsula is yours to discover.

Capital City

Doha

Qatar's extraordinary capital — where a soaring futuristic skyline frames a historic waterfront corniche, world-class museums sit steps from ancient souqs, and the energy of a global city pulses through every neighbourhood.

1.2M+ residents Central east coast 10 min from airport
Discover Doha
City of the Future

Lusail

Qatar's purpose-built second city — FIFA World Cup Final stadium, Formula 1 circuit, Lusail Marina, and a dazzling waterfront promenade.

Explore Lusail
Nature & Heritage

Al Khor

Mangroves, flamingos, ancient watchtowers, Al Bayt Stadium, and the best kayaking in Qatar — all 45 minutes from Doha.

Explore Al Khor
Heritage Town

Al Wakrah

Qatar's most authentic heritage town — with a restored souq, pearl-diving village, 17th-century mosque and Al Janoub Stadium.

Explore Al Wakrah
Education & Sport

Al Rayyan

Home to Education City, Qatar Foundation, Aspire Zone, Ahmad Bin Ali Stadium, and Mall of Qatar — Qatar's second most populous municipality.

Explore Al Rayyan
Oil & Beaches

Dukhan

Where oil was first struck in 1939 — today a gateway to Qatar's wild west coast, dramatic cliffs, pristine beaches and fossil-rich desert.

Explore Dukhan
UNESCO World Heritage

Al Zubarah

A UNESCO World Heritage Site — preserved ruins of an 18th-century pearling town and a spectacular desert fort in Qatar's remote northwest.

Explore Al Zubarah
Off the Beaten Track

Quiet Towns Worth Exploring

Madinat Al Shamal

Qatar's northernmost settlement, surrounded by pristine, uncrowded beaches and abandoned fishing villages frozen in time.

Discover

Al Ruwais

A quiet coastal town with Qatar's oldest mosque (17th century) and access to the remarkable Al Jassasiya petroglyphs.

Discover

Mesaieed

Small industrial town between Doha and Al Wakrah, famed for dune bashing, the Inland Sea gateway, and Sealine Beach.

Discover

Al Shahaniya

Qatar's camel racing heartland — where ancient racing traditions continue every winter at the Al Shahaniya Camel Racing Track.

Qatar Peninsula — City Locations

Qatar's Cities at a Glance

Qatar is compact enough to explore its entire peninsula in a single road trip. Every city is within 1–2 hours of Doha by car, and the expanding Doha Metro and bus network makes many destinations accessible without a vehicle.

  • Doha — Capital
  • Lusail — 23 km N
  • Al Khor — 60 km N
  • Al Wakrah — 25 km S
  • Al Rayyan — 10 km W
  • Dukhan — 80 km W
  • Al Zubarah — 105 km NW
  • Al Shamal — 130 km N
