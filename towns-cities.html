Qatar's Cities and Towns

Each settlement in Qatar carries its own story, character, and attractions worth exploring.

Capital

Doha

The beating heart of Qatar, Doha is a dynamic metropolis combining cutting-edge architecture with traditional souqs and world-class cultural institutions.

Learn More
Heritage

Al Wakrah

One of Qatar's oldest fishing villages, Al Wakrah retains much of its traditional charm with a beautifully restored souq and a picturesque waterfront.

Nature

Al Khor

A tranquil coastal town north of Doha, Al Khor is beloved for its mangrove forests, flamingo sightings, and peaceful dhow harbour.

Historical

Al Zubarah

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Al Zubarah's ancient fort and archaeological site recall Qatar's mercantile past in the 18th and 19th centuries.

Education

Al Rayyan

Home to Education City and the magnificent Aspire Zone, Al Rayyan is Qatar's city of learning, sports, and innovation.

Coastal

Dukhan

Perched on Qatar's western coast, Dukhan offers breathtaking Gulf sunsets, blue holes for diving, and proximity to the stunning inland desert.
