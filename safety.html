Safety in Qatar

Qatar is consistently ranked among the safest countries in the world for residents and visitors alike.

Ranked #1Safest Country - Global Index
A Safe Destination

Qatar's Exceptional Safety Record

Qatar has consistently ranked as one of the safest countries in the world across multiple global indices. The country's low crime rate, strong rule of law, and well-resourced emergency services provide visitors with an exceptional level of security and peace of mind.

Visitors will notice a visible and courteous police presence throughout Doha and major tourist areas. Qatar's approach to public safety is proactive and visitor-friendly, with tourist police officers available to assist with any concerns.

Safety Information

Key Safety Tips for Visitors

Healthcare

Qatar's healthcare system is excellent. Hamad Medical Corporation operates world-class hospitals. Travel insurance with healthcare coverage is recommended.

Emergency Numbers

Police: 999. Ambulance: 999. Fire: 999. All emergency services are accessible via a single number. Tourist Police: +974 4444 1234.

Heat Safety

Qatar's summer temperatures can exceed 45°C. Stay hydrated, seek shade during peak afternoon hours, and use high-factor sunscreen at all times.

Cultural Respect

Dress modestly in public areas and religious sites. Public displays of affection should be avoided. Alcohol is available only in licensed venues.

Road Safety

Drive defensively and observe speed limits carefully. Seatbelts are compulsory for all occupants. Mobile phone use while driving is illegal.

Water Safety

Tap water in Qatar is safe to drink but many visitors prefer bottled water. Be cautious in the sea during strong winds and always heed lifeguard warnings.

Travel with Confidence

Qatar is one of the world's safest destinations. Enjoy your visit with complete peace of mind.
