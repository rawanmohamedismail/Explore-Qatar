An Introduction

Qatar: The Pearl of the Gulf

Qatar is a sovereign Arab state situated on a small peninsula in the northeastern Arabian Peninsula. Bordered by Saudi Arabia to the south and surrounded by the Persian Gulf on all other sides, Qatar has emerged as one of the most prosperous and ambitious nations in the world.

Rich in oil and natural gas, Qatar has leveraged its wealth to build world-class infrastructure, cultural institutions, and sporting facilities that are the envy of the region. Yet despite its modernity, Qatar has remained deeply committed to its Bedouin heritage and Islamic traditions.
