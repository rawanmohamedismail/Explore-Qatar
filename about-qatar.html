About Qatar

A Land of Infinite Wonders

Qatar is a young destination with a rich heritage that will leave you wanting more. It's where the desert sands meet the ocean and where ancient traditions and modern wonders live side by side. Pronounced 'kuh-TAR', this small peninsula on the Arabian Gulf has become one of the world's most captivating destinations — a place that surprises, inspires, and stays with you long after you leave.

From the iconic skyline of Doha to the silent, rolling dunes of the Inland Sea, from the world-class museums of Qatar Museums to the warm hospitality of its people — Qatar is a destination unlike any other.
