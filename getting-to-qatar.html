Getting to Qatar

Qatar is superbly connected to the world through its award-winning national carrier and Hamad International Airport.

Best AirlineWorld's Best 2024
Flying to Qatar

Qatar Airways — Your Gateway

Qatar Airways, the award-winning national carrier of Qatar, connects Doha's Hamad International Airport to over 170 destinations across six continents. Consistently ranked among the world's finest airlines, Qatar Airways makes your journey to Qatar a pleasure in itself.

Hamad International Airport (HIA) is one of the world's busiest and most celebrated transit hubs, regularly winning awards for its design, retail, and passenger experience. Direct flights to Doha are available from virtually every major city in the world.

Travel Information

How to Reach Qatar

By Air

The most common way to reach Qatar is by air. Hamad International Airport handles over 45 million passengers annually with connections to 170+ destinations worldwide.

By Sea

Luxury cruise ships dock at Doha's cruise terminal. Qatar is a popular port of call on Gulf itineraries, with several major cruise lines calling regularly.

By Road

Qatar is accessible by road from Saudi Arabia via the Abu Samra border crossing. The planned Qatar-Bahrain causeway will further improve road connectivity.

Flight Times

London to Doha: approx. 7 hours. New York to Doha: approx. 12 hours. Sydney to Doha: approx. 14 hours. Mumbai to Doha: approx. 3 hours.

Baggage Allowance

Qatar Airways offers generous baggage allowances. Economy class passengers typically receive 23kg of checked luggage plus 7kg hand luggage.

In-Flight Entertainment

Qatar Airways' Oryx One entertainment system offers thousands of hours of entertainment on ultra-high-definition screens across all cabin classes.
