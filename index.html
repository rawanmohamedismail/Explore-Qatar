Why Visit Qatar

A Destination Like No Other

Qatar blends the warmth of Arabian hospitality with world-class infrastructure — creating an experience unlike anywhere on earth.

Safe & Welcoming

Qatar consistently ranks among the safest destinations in the world, with a warm and hospitable culture that embraces visitors from every nation.

95+ Nations enjoy visa-free access
World-Class Hospitality

From five-star hotels to boutique desert camps, Qatar offers extraordinary accommodation for every style of traveller.

55+ Hotels and resorts
Effortless to Explore

Qatar is compact enough to experience fully in a short visit, with world-class transport links and Hamad International Airport connecting you globally.

160+ Destinations connected
Year-Round Sunshine

With 365 days of sunshine, Qatar is always ready to welcome you. The cooler months from October to April offer ideal weather for outdoor exploration.

365 Days of sunshine annually
Rich Culture & Heritage

Discover museums of global significance, ancient forts, vibrant arts districts and living traditions that have shaped this remarkable nation for centuries.

7,000+ Years of civilisation
Extraordinary Cuisine

Qatar's dining scene spans the globe — from fragrant Qatari machbous to Michelin-starred restaurants, street food souqs to rooftop dining with bay views.

300+ Dining experiences
