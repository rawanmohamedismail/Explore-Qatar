Travel Tips for Qatar

Practical advice and insider knowledge to help you make the most of your Qatari adventure.

Essential Tips

Everything You Need to Know

Prepare for your Qatar visit with these practical tips from our team of local experts.

Weather

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Qatar is between October and April when temperatures are pleasantly mild (18–28°C). Summer months are extremely hot but offer discounted hotel rates.

Culture

Cultural Etiquette

Dress modestly when visiting souqs, mosques, and government buildings. A light scarf or shawl is recommended for women. Remove shoes before entering mosques.

Money

Currency & Payments

The Qatari Riyal (QAR) is the official currency. Credit cards are widely accepted. ATMs are available throughout Doha. Tipping 10–15% is customary in restaurants.

Connectivity

Staying Connected

Qatar has excellent 5G coverage and free WiFi in many public spaces, malls, and the Metro. Local SIM cards are available from Ooredoo and Vodafone Qatar at the airport.

Communication

Language

Arabic is the official language but English is widely spoken throughout Doha. Menus, signs, and Metro information are all available in English.

Packing

What to Pack

Light, breathable clothing for outdoors. A light jacket for heavily air-conditioned malls and restaurants. Modest clothing for religious and traditional sites.
