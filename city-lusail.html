Overview

Qatar's Vision of Tomorrow

Lusail is one of the most ambitious urban development projects in the world — a meticulously planned city rising from desert and sea just 23 km north of Doha. Built to eventually house 200,000 residents with another 250,000 planned, Lusail represents Qatar's vision of what a 21st-century city can be: smart, sustainable, beautifully designed and spectacularly connected.

The city was thrust into the global spotlight when it hosted the 2022 FIFA World Cup Final at Lusail Stadium — an 80,000-seat architectural masterpiece whose golden latticed exterior was inspired by traditional Arabic lamps and Fanar Islamic art. The stadium sits at the heart of the city, surrounded by the entertainment district of Lusail Marina, the Formula 1 Lusail International Circuit, and an expanding network of parks, waterfront promenades, towers, and cultural spaces.

Lusail is divided into 19 distinctive districts, each with its own character — from the upscale Fox Hills residential towers to the vibrant dining scene of Lusail Marina Boulevard, the serene waterfront walks of Entertainment City, and the busy commercial towers of Lusail City Centre. A Light Rail Transit system and connection to the Doha Metro Red Line make it easily accessible.

Did You Know?

Lusail International Circuit has hosted the Formula One Qatar Grand Prix since 2021 — always as a spectacular night race under floodlights. It is one of only a handful of circuits in the world built to host both MotoGP and Formula One.

Top Attractions in Lusail

Sport

Lusail Stadium

The 80,000-seat golden arena that hosted the 2022 FIFA World Cup Final. Its iconic latticed facade is inspired by traditional Islamic art. One of the world's most beautiful sports venues.

Sport

Lusail F1 Circuit

The Lusail International Circuit hosts the Formula One Qatar Grand Prix — always at night, under 3,600 floodlights. Also home to MotoGP and other international motorsport events.

Beach

Lusail Marina

A stunning marina lined with restaurants, cafés, promenades and waterfront bars. The energy of the city converges here — especially on weekend evenings.

Culture

Place Vendôme Mall

A luxury shopping and lifestyle destination inspired by Paris's Place Vendôme — with designer boutiques, a retractable roof, indoor canals, and fine dining.

Culture

Lusail Entertainment City

A family-friendly entertainment zone with theme parks, cinemas, go-kart tracks, laser tag, and outdoor event spaces along the waterfront.

Beach

Lusail Waterfront

The city's beautiful north waterfront — perfect for evening walks, cycling, watercraft, and watching the lights of the towers reflect in the Gulf waters.

Getting to Lusail

By Metro

Take the Doha Metro Red Line North to the Lusail QNB station. Journey from downtown Doha takes approximately 35 minutes. Affordable and air-conditioned.

By Car

Lusail is 23 km north of central Doha via the Lusail Expressway. Journey takes 20–30 minutes depending on traffic. Ample parking throughout the city.

By Bus

Bus routes connect Lusail to Doha and onward to Al Khor. The Lusail Bus Station at Lusail QNB Metro provides regional connections.
