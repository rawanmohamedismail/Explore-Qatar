Retail Paradise

Shopping in Qatar

From vast luxury malls to vibrant traditional souqs, Qatar offers an exceptional shopping experience.

Tax FreeShopping Paradise
Shopping Destination

Qatar's Extraordinary Retail Scene

Qatar offers one of the finest shopping experiences in the Middle East, combining cutting-edge luxury retail centres with the timeless magic of traditional souqs. The country's tax-free status means excellent value across all product categories.

Doha Festival City, Villaggio Mall, Mall of Qatar, and Place Vendôme Qatar are among the most spectacular shopping centres in the region, housing hundreds of international and luxury brands alongside outstanding dining and entertainment options.

Shopping Destinations

Where to Shop in Qatar

Discover Qatar's most exciting and diverse shopping destinations.

Traditional

Souq Waqif

Qatar's most beloved traditional market, filled with fragrant spices, handicrafts, textiles, falcons, and traditional Qatari goods in a beautifully restored setting.

Luxury

The Pearl-Qatar

Luxury boutiques, international designer brands, and artisan shops line the elegant promenades of this stunning artificial island development.

Mall

Mall of Qatar

One of Qatar's largest shopping malls featuring over 500 stores, an indoor snow park, IMAX cinema, and an extraordinary dining plaza.

Fashion

Place Vendôme Qatar

Inspired by Paris's iconic Place Vendôme, this magnificent mall houses some of the world's most prestigious luxury fashion and lifestyle brands.

Art

Qatar Art Souq

Browse and purchase original artworks, prints, and crafts by Qatari and regional artists at this unique cultural shopping destination.

Gifts

Qatari Handicrafts

Bring home authentic Qatari handicrafts including woven textiles, traditional pottery, camel leather goods, and beautiful Arabic calligraphy pieces.
