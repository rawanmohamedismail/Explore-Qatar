Cultural Experiences

Art & Culture in Qatar

Qatar is home to some of the world's most extraordinary museums, galleries, and cultural institutions.

QMQatar Museums Authority
Cultural Capital

Qatar's World-Class Culture Scene

Qatar has invested billions in creating cultural institutions that rank among the finest in the world. The Museum of Islamic Art, designed by I.M. Pei, the National Museum of Qatar shaped like a desert rose, and the extraordinary Mathaf Arab Museum of Modern Art all attract cultural visitors from across the globe.

Beyond museums, Qatar's cultural calendar is packed with art biennales, international film festivals, literary events, and the colourful celebrations of national holidays that provide windows into Qatar's living heritage.

Cultural Destinations

Where to Find Culture in Qatar

Explore Qatar's extraordinary range of museums, galleries, and cultural spaces.

Museum

Museum of Islamic Art

The MIA houses one of the world's finest collections of Islamic art and artefacts spanning 14 centuries, displayed in I.M. Pei's iconic waterfront building.

Learn More
Architecture

National Museum of Qatar

Designed by Jean Nouvel in the form of a desert rose crystal, Qatar's national museum tells the story of the land, people, and nation of Qatar.

Learn More
Contemporary

Mathaf Modern Art

The Arab Museum of Modern Art in Education City houses the world's most significant collection of modern and contemporary Arab art.

Learn More
Performing Arts

Katara Cultural Village

Qatar's premier cultural venue hosts theatre, opera, cinema, art exhibitions, and the celebrated Katara International Film Festival.

Learn More
Creative

Fire Station Artist Residency

Qatar's thriving contemporary art scene is anchored by the Fire Station, providing studios and exhibition space for emerging local artists.

Learn More
Heritage

Msheireb Museums

Four beautifully restored merchant houses in Doha's heart tell the story of Qatari society, identity, and the transformation of the nation.
